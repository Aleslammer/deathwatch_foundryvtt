Description

Brother Artemis stands as a towering exemplar of the Blood Angels Chapter, his crimson armor adorned with purity seals earned through countless battles against the enemies of mankind. His eyes burn with barely contained fury, a testament to the Red Thirst that flows through his veins.

Past Events

Inducted into the Blood Angels during the defense of Baal against Hive Fleet Leviathan. Served with distinction in the 5th Company before being selected for Deathwatch service. Notable engagements include the Purge of Ghenna Prime and the defense of Watch Fortress Erioch.
