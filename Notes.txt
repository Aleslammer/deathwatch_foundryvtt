The Kroot are a species of almost limitless variety, having travelled countless evolutionary paths. While the form most associated with the species is referred to by the Imperium as the Kroot Carnivore, it is postulated that almost every single animal life form on their home world of Pech is related, albeit distantly, to the genus. Evolution in the Kroot has become a matter of rapid adaptation. The Kroot evolve so quickly that they are in danger of overtaking their own development and becoming so specialised that they are led down dead-end evolutionary paths. This has happened to many members of the genus, who have devolved into creatures only able to exist in a very specific environment or to eat only a particular prey. Even the slightest alteration in environmental factors can result in the extinction of these hyper-specialised sub-species
